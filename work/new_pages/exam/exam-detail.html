What is IAS Exam?

IAS (Indian Administrative Services) formerly known as Imperial Civil Service (ICS) is a bureaucracy of India. Through the IAS (UPSC CSE) exam, the conducting body recruits candidates for the All-India Services such as Indian Administrative Services (IAS), Indian Police Service (IPS), IRS, etc. respectively. Although regarded as one of the toughest exams in the country, with the right approach and strategy, an aspirant can crack the IAS examination in the very first attempt. The IAS exam is conducted in three phases that are Prelims, Mains, and Interview round.Those who clear all the three stages of the IAS exam enter into the prestigious civil services of the country, and become officers in the Indian Administrative Service (IAS), Indian Police Service (IPS), Indian Foreign Service (IFS) and a host of other services.
